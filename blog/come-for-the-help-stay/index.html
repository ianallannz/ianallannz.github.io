Come for the help, stay for the rewilding #

Wait, stop right there! #

Before we go any further, this is so 90's. I've been re-reading an old David Carson book of mine recently. Carson says some important things about professional intuition that I will explore in future posts.

Experiencing that book and Carson's work again inspired me to try algorithmically creating Carson-like layouts. So that's what you're seeing here with "So 90's"; it's early days.

Back to business... #

Welcome to my new corner on the web!

I'm here and you're here to see where I can help professionally. I do do extraordinary ordinary work and you'll have also noticed that this site is a bit of an off-kilter experience.

I'm one of those OG web creators who emerged as the web was invented by the glorious Sir Tim Berners-Lee. Read his book!

In love with the human spirit #

For a quite while now I've lamented how the very human eccentricities in web design, graphic design, and even interior design have been mostly neutered by social media and SEO algorithms (and now LLMs) in the name of efficiency and homogenisation.

We're also losing our resiliency and our sovereignty online. It's nothing new to say we've become dependent on bigtech platforms, often compulsively, even resentfully.

So while I'm helping you professionally in an extraordinary ordinary way, I'll also be pursuing a rewilding of the internet, definitely personally, but all going well as a commercial venture too.

Thank God it's Monday #

Thanks for wading down here to experience a little rewilding of the web.

Meanwhile, I'll see you out there for the extraordinary ordinary; let's talk!
